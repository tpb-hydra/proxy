How do I download? 

This guide will show you how to download from The Pirate Bay.

Torrent client

Installing

  1. Download your prefered BitTorrent client
  2. Install it.

Download

  1. Go to thepiratebay and select a download. Click "Get this torrent".
  2. Now your web browser might ask you what application you want to open the file with. Select your BitTorrent client and make sure the browser remembers your choise.
  3. Your files are now being downloaded.

Problems, questions?

Q: How do I select which files to download within a torrent using magnets?
A: Magnets are torrents, only the torrents are downloaded from the swarm of peers directly by your torrent client, not from TPB. So wait a few seconds till your client has connected and downloaded the torrent file with the metadata. Now choose your torrent in Bitlord and in the Files tab select which files to download or not. Here's a video to explain it all: youtube.com/watch?v=KCIclko7Bc8

Q: Magnets doens't open in my bittorrent client
A: The easiest way to fix this is to uninstall and then install you BitTorrent client again.
Or fix your settings like this: youtube.com/watch?v=gpBN9cDObDU
